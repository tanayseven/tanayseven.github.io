About me

I am passionate about software engineering from a very young age. It all started from age of 3 when I used to play games on a computer which was a DOS computer. I eventually got obssesed with becoming a software engineer. Around the age of 17 I got a chance to run my first "Hello World" program in C and then never stopped programming. I don't just do programming for a living but I also do a lot of programming at free time or as a hobby. I love reading up about things related to science/tech and space and watch videos on YouTube about the same. I am a wannabe polymath and probably will be one throughout my life.

I love writing programs which are games. So if you wanna collaborate, you can ask me and I would love to pick up and work on a project. I'm also a huge follower of good programming practices and good engineering practices and I love to advocate them. A big lover of declarative/functional languages, but okay to work with almost any languages.
